Tofu Soup

Description

This is a popular Korean dish best served during cold, winter months. It consists of a ton of veggies, tofu, and meat. The dish is traditionally served in a hot, stone pot. But, for the purposes of this recipe, we will just be using one large pot.

Ingredients

  • Silken Tofu
  • Gochugaru
  • Sesame Oil
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Pork Belly
  • Eggs
  • Bonito Stock
  • Tsuyu

Tofu Soup

Description

This is a popular Korean dish best served during cold, winter months. It consists of a ton of veggies, tofu, and meat. The dish is traditionally served in a hot, stone pot. But, for the purposes of this recipe, we will just be using one large pot.

Ingredients

  • Silken Tofu
  • Gochugaru
  • Sesame Oil
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Pork Belly
  • Eggs
  • Bonito Stock
  • Tsuyu

Steps

  1. Place pot on medium heat
  2. Add sesame oil, diced onions, and minced garlic
  3. When the alliums have browned a bit, add the pork belly
  4. After the pork has done cooking, add gochugaru and soy sauce
  5. Add the bonito stock
  6. Bring the soup into a boil, then add the tofu
  7. Add tsuyu to your taste
  8. When the soup has done cooking, add a bit more sesame oil and crack the egg on top

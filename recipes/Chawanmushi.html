Chawanmushi

Description

This is a popular Japanese dish best served as a side, appetizer, or breakfast. It's a very simple, quick, and delicious dish to make.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Tsuyu
  • Water

Steps

  1. Create a tsuyu stock, use 1 cup of water with 2 table spoons of tsuyu sauce
  2. Whisk your eggs and add the stock
  3. Strain the mixture into a steamable pot
  4. Cover the pot with aluminum foil
  5. Steam for 20 minutes over low heat
